Hi, I'm Dom!

An aspiring full stack web developer, eager to learn at every step of the way!

I developed an interest in web development while starting at my first job. The work came in at a snail's pace. Hoping to make the most of this free time, I completed a small project through freecodecamp's website. After finishing freeOnlineCodeCamp’s course, I earned my certification in web development from the University of Massachusetts Lowell. Having a limited understanding of website creation from my undergraduate studies, the certificate grounded my knowledge of application development, but overall lacked in providing me with a high-level overview of web development technologies.

With a desire to deepen my understanding of web development, I am now pursuing a graduate degree at Boston University's Metropolitan College’s online master of science program in computer information systems with a concentration in web application development, I strive to gain the holistic theoretical, and practical, web development knowledge necessary to have a competitive advantage in the job market.
